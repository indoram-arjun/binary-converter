About This Tool

This web application is designed to help students, tech enthusiasts, and curious minds learn how binary encoding works in a simple and interactive way. Just type your text, convert it to binary, hear it through speech synthesis, and even share it with your friends using the Web Share API!

Whether you're a coder or just someone having fun, this tool is built for you. Made with ❤️ for learning and exploring the digital world.
